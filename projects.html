My projects investigate simulation methods and develop mechanisms for low-power wide-area networks. You can find all public repositories on my global GitHub profile: github.com/sanoufrancois226-crypto.

LoRaFlexSim

Modular event-driven simulator for LoRa and LoRaWAN networks, designed to evaluate protocol behavior at scale. I lead architecture design and implementation with a focus on reproducibility and extensibility.

Implementing LoRaWAN End-Device Classes A, B, and C in LoRaFlexSim
