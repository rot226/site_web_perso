Projects

My work spans the development of simulation frameworks and research into new mechanisms for low‑power wide‑area networks.

  • LoRaFlexSim – a modular, event‑driven simulator for LoRa and LoRaWAN networks. The project focuses on fidelity, extensibility and ease of use. I lead the design and implementation of this tool. Visit the project website.
  • Implementing LoRaWAN End‑Device Classes A, B, and C in LoRaFlexSim – a project that extends LoRaFlexSim to fully support all LoRaWAN device classes and explores their performance in various scenarios. Read the paper.
  • Adaptive Algorithms for LPWANs – ongoing research into intelligent resource management and adaptive data rate schemes to improve network efficiency and device lifetime.
